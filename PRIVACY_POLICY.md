# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** 05/03/2026

## 1. Introduction

Travel Map Vietnam ("we", "our", "us") values your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when using our mobile application.

---

## 2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following information:

* **Location Data (GPS):**
  Used to show your current position on the map and provide navigation features.

* **Account Information:**
  Email address and login information when you sign in.

* **Device Information:**
  Basic device details such as device type and operating system.

---

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

* Provide map and navigation features
* Display your location
* Improve app performance
* Authenticate users (login)

---

## 4. Data Sharing

We do NOT sell your personal data.

We may share data with:

* **Firebase (Google)** for authentication and storage
* Authorities if required by law

## 5. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your data, but no system is completely secure.

## 6. User Control

You can:
